What We Do

Services Tailored
to Every Garden

Whether you need a regular tidy-up or a complete garden redesign, we've got you covered — with the skills, equipment, and local know-how to get the job done right.

🌿

Garden Maintenance

Keep your garden looking its best all year round. Our regular maintenance packages cover weeding, pruning, border tidying, planting, and seasonal clean-ups — so your outdoor space always makes the right impression without you having to lift a finger.

🏡

Landscaping

Dreaming of a garden that truly works for you? Our landscaping service takes your ideas and turns them into reality. From patios and pathways to planting schemes and garden design, we work with you to create outdoor spaces that are both beautiful and practical.

✂️

Hedge Cutting

Neat, shaped hedges make all the difference to a garden's kerb appeal. We carefully trim and shape all types of hedging — from box and privet to laurel and leylandii — leaving clean, crisp lines and healthy growth. No job too small or too large.

🌱

Lawn Mowing

A lush, well-cut lawn is the foundation of any great garden. We offer flexible one-off cuts or regular mowing schedules to keep your grass at its best throughout the growing season. We'll also edge and tidy borders for that finishing touch.

🏘️

Communal Gardening

We work closely with housing associations, property management companies, and residents' groups to maintain communal green spaces across Surrey. Reliable, discreet, and professionally managed — we keep shared gardens looking smart for everyone who uses them.
